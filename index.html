Scoring Rules

1. Player will win if he gets higher points.

2. Winner Player Score = Winner Player Score + (Winner Player Score - Lost Player Score)

Double Roll Round Scoring System

If one player rolls a '1' and another player rolls a '6', then no one gets any points for that round. However, the next round will be a Double Roll Round, or simply a DR Round. In this round, the winning player will earn double points (2x). If the roll results in '1' and '6' again during this round, the following two rounds will be DR Rounds, and so on.
